Auto Attendant

An auto-attendant is a prerecorded “menu” system that can be used to direct callers to different departments or employees within your company. After your setup is complete you can use this feature to let your callers listen to commonly requested information, such as your business address, hours of operation, and more.

You can use it to streamline your call flow and present your callers with a professional-sounding system. The VOXO auto-attendant feature is highly customizable and can be modified and scaled to meet the needs of nearly any business application. Your VOXO includes several auto-attendant and options which you can easily customize through our VOXO cloud PBX interface.

While callers are waiting to be connected to a department or are waiting on hold for the next available representative, they can hear music or promotional messages. You can also use your telephone to place a caller on hold at any time during a conversation. Managing your custom music or promotional information is simple through our powerful Hosted PBX web interface. You can change your on-hold music track at any time, upload new tracks, or even adjust the volume of a particular track.

ALWAYS IN TOUCH

With dedicated line plans that provide you with unlimited calling capacity, both inbound and outbound, customers will never hear a busy signal. Rest assured your staff will be able to handle even an unexpectedly high volume of calls. These plans are the ideal choice for busy, phone-intensive offices, and also for call centers and contact centers of all sizes.

You will also be able to create “rules” that define your business hours. With this feature, you can easily set the days and times during which callers can reach your staff via phone. Using business hour rules you can also create different phone call behaviors based on day and time.

VOXO provides mobility options that let you select multiple locations to dial when someone calls you. If you were to specify a cell phone, your office business phone and a telephone at another branch office the system will call all these telephones at the same time, or in sequence if you prefer. Now you can work out of the office and still receive calls normally to any location or device you configure.
