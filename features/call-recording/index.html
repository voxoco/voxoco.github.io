Call Recording

VOXO offers a comprehensive call recording solution. Our solutions allow you to record 100 percent of your calls, archive them efficiently, and find specific calls quickly and easily. VOXO call recording allows you to listen at any time to the audio of a previous phone call. This feature is very useful for a number of scenarios, including: reviewing lengthy or detailed calls; demonstrating compliance with regulatory requirements; or assisting in settling disagreements over the content of a conversation.

At VOXO we take a simplified approach to capturing and maintaining calls. As more and more companies recognize the need to record all calls, we take the hassle out of this business-critical endeavor. No more wasted time looking for recordings. Advanced search options and powerful data tagging let you search among hundreds or even thousands of logged interactions and play back relevant recordings quickly and easily.

The recording software can be secured to only allow authorized users, supervisors and manager access to the customer area respecting security and legal constrains. Full support for Secure Sockets Layer (SSL/HTTPS) connections, 256-bit AES encryption, and securely partitioned resources means rock-solid client security and data integrity.
