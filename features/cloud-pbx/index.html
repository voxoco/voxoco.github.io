CLOUD PBX

A hosted IP-PBX from VOXO includes the ability for businesses to make and receive phone calls over the internet, route incoming calls to the right individuals or teams inside the company, and adds standard standard business phone system features like an auto-attendant or virtual receptionist, business voicemail, call transfers between employees, and more.

Cloud PBX gives you access to PBX capabilities without the need for complicated and expensive equipment. Cloud PBX is a hosted or virtual PBX, meaning that all of the routing services are performed by VOXO, rather than by an employee managing a PBX system in your office.

FEATURES AND SAVINGS

In comparison, a cloud PBX more than outmatches an on-site PBX for feature set. More importantly, cloud technology greatly improves upon the PBX concept, and resolves the pain points of its technological ancestor.

Here are just a few of the many advantages of a cloud-based PBX from VOXO:

  • Greatly reduced initial capital outlay: there is no “box” to purchase
  • Reduced ongoing costs: clients can easily self-manage a cloud PBX
  • Highly flexible: thanks to cloud technology, your PBX can be reconfigured at a moment’s notice
  • Reliability: a cloud PBX can run on multiple separate servers, meaning there is no single point of failure
