UNIFIED COMMUNICATIONS

VoIP is quickly becoming the standard for managing communications in across all sectors. Like other technologies such as Internet access and Web presence it’s likely this technology will eventually become the standard throughout the business world. With the ability to tie together voice, video, messaging and fax, VOXO VoIP can provide your organization with the benefits of a unified system.

Unifying your communications with VOXO can greatly increase productivity, especially in companies that rely on daily (or more frequent) communications with customers, partners, vendors, and within the company.

Users can monitor their calls, choosing which ones to answer immediately and which to send to the voice mail system. In addition, they can access messages via their e-mail accounts using POP, IMAP, or HTTP (i.e., Web-based mail). Other unifying features of VOXO VoIP include:

  • Using text-to-speech translation to have e-mail messages read to the user over the phone.
  • Ability to answer e-mail messages by dictating a reply over the phone.
  • View or forward fax messages from an e-mail client on any computer, phone  or mobile device.
  • Get notification of new voice mail messages, e-mail messages, or faxes via pager or cell phone.
  • Work from remote or mobile locations without losing any features
