VOIP CONVERSIONS

Today, a business phone system is much more than just a simple switch. Adjunct technologies like automated attendant, voice messaging, call queuing and multi-party conferencing have become standard features.

Basic analog and proprietary digital phones are giving way to standards-based IP phones. Outside connectivity is now available over the Internet in the form of SIP trunks or other VoIP services.

Setup and installation is generally quick and painless. VOXO will conduct an initial review of your existing telephone structure and the make a custom solution for your situation.

Once planning is complete, on site implementation will begin. Most on site installations are completed in a workday, but can take longer depending on the size and scope.

After physical deployment the customization and software phase begins. During this time, extensions are assigned, call groups crated and new voicemail boxes and routing are configured. Once complete, your new VOIP system will be up and running which allows you to start saving money, offering better service and focusing on your goals.

COST SAVINGS

When PBXs were originally developed, wireline phone calls were the only type of electronic communication available. Today, the communications landscape has expanded to include email, instant messaging, video conferencing, desktop sharing, SMS and mobile telephony.

Countless businesses are turning to IP PBX solutions over legacy PBX systems because they are cheaper to install and maintain. Between labor and maintenance the cost savings are often realized in the first month of service.

Implementing a VOIP Unified Communications solution will increase efficiency while simplifying management, and with VOXO’s ongoing world-class customer support you’ll have resources available for the life of your system.
