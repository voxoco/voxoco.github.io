BUSINESS PHONE SYSTEMS

Across all business sectors, VoIP, or voice over Internet Protocol, is getting a lot of attention. In essence,  VoIP combines voice and data together on a single, secure network foundation.\

With VOXO VoIP, your communications system can handle anything your enterprise requires. You get inclusive, global-ready capabilities that are flexible, simple to use and scale on demand; fortified solutions built to exacting federal security and compliance standards; high reliability to meet demanding SLAs, and easy administration that gives you complete control.

FEATURE RICH

For small and medium-sized buisnesses VoIP allows you to project a big-company image on a small company budget. With enterprise features like auto attendant, music-on-hold, web conferencing, web faxing and video meetings. Your employees and customers will benefit as well:

  • Easy, straight-forward browser-based management
  • Real-time monitors and system reports
  • Virtual Receptionists, Voicemail to Email, Email to Fax and many features not available on traditional PBX systems.
  • Your employees will have more ways to stay connected wherever they are.
  • Your customers can more easily reach your employees, without getting frustrated by lengthy phone system menus or by being sent to voicemail.
  • Using ‘presence’ technology, you’ll be able to quickly determine who on your staff is available and the best way to contact them

 

COST SAVING

Hosted VoIP from VOXO allows you to do away with your expensive PBX hardware and eliminates the need to maintain idle telecom circuit capacity.

A cloud-based PBX takes this concept yet further, removing the central hardware infrastructure from the premise and leaving behind a small footprint of VoIP phones and adapters, and providing a standard API for application access.

With VOXO VoiP  it’s easy to add, move, or change phone connections to accommodate a growing and increasingly mobile workforce. We offer a deep, robust, feature set that can be deployed quickly. All backed by our top-class customer support.

With the ability to  take advantage of all the traditional features you’ve come to expect from your phone system, all at no additional cost, you will also dramatically save on phone system and long distance charges. VOXO VoIP can help your business take the next step.
