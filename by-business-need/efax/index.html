EFAX

Across all sectors, managing costs is vital. Features like VOXO EFAX make it easy to simple to send and receive faxes without the added expense and ongoing maintenance of a traditional fax machine. Also, you’ll never have to stand in line to fax something again, as everyone in your organization can have individual fax numbers.

Fax over VoIP refers to the process of sending and receiving faxes over an IP network instead of the public telephone network. The key benefit of sending faxes in this manner is that it saves money on long-distance charges. When a user sends a fax over IP, a fax machine transmits the image data as packets through the public Internet instead of over the public telephone network.

VOXO EFAX also allows you to receive your faxes on either your desktop or smartphone, eliminating the need to wait around for a promised fax’s arrival. It’s all managed with your voicemail and emails, unified in one inbox. Additionally, Paperless Fax lets you send faxes to your contacts while on the go. It’s a true mobile solution for a traditional element of business.
