RELIABILITY & UPTIME

Reliability matters. With VOXO VoIP it’s easier to be reliable and available. Our systems are co-located to ensure resources are always available for you and your organization. Simply put, downtime or being out of touch isn’t an option. With more effective communications you will be able to quickly and efficiently correspond with clients, business partners, employees and suppliers. Our infrastructure co-located and cloud based providing you with the dependability you need, when you need it.

REDUNDANT

  • Redundant Internet connections. VOXO implements multiple routers  that aggregate two or more broadband connections from different providers to provide more bandwidth and to automatically failover when one connection goes down.
  • Power backup (UPS). Our power backup system will continue supplying electrical power to your VoIP equipment and Internet routers if there’s a power failure.
  • Multiple Data Centers. Our infrastructure is co-located and provided with battery backup so there is always an offsite location available in case of an outage to provide services.
