ONE MANAGEMENT PORTAL

VOXO knows that your organization is not static. Conditions are constantly changing and your phone system must be able to adapt to meet new goals and anticipate changes in market conditions. VOXO’s management portal makes it easy for authorized users to administer your system and for users to set their own phone system preferences.

  • Brilliantly simple plug-and-play deployment—changing or temporary location setup is quick and easy
  • Manage call groups, users, voicemail boxes and recordings from one interface.
  • Easily scale your operations up or down to fit business seasonality and growth
  • Maximize uptime and business continuity with a service and support backed by an iron-clad Service Level Agreement (SLA)

PRODUCTIVITY IN THE CLOUD

Seamlessly access to customers, partners, suppliers, and coworkers from virtually any location. Your employees will be able to work as easily from the road as they can in the office. They can access data, voice-mails and more from virtually anywhere.

  • Users can define personal call routing rules for their phones and mobile devices
  • Receive all e-mails, voice-mails, faxes, text messages and more all in one central internet-connected location.
  • Simplify collaboration with coworkers. See which employees are available and their best method of contact at specified times.
  • Employees can be instantly added to contact center queues, and automatic overflowing during busy periods reduces staff costs and improves service
