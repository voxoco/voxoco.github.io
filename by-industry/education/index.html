EDUCATION

Schools across the country are cutting costs and minimizing downtime by moving to Voice over IP (VoIP) phone service that has been customized for education.

VOXO offers schools and libraries the same low costs and advanced features that homes and business customers experience.

  • Replace multiple PBXes and key systems, simplifying administration and management without sacrificing reliability or voice quality
  • Eliminate the need to pay for upgrades or to “wait for someone come to fix the phones” again!
  • Receive VOIP advantages that traditional phone systems lack—such as voicemail-to-email, web conferencing and Internet faxing.

 

K12 school systems across the country are leading the VoIP charge and could teach a lot of the business sectors a thing or two about the efficiency, flexibility, and costeffectiveness of next-generation voice platforms. VoIP is proving to be the most cost-effective way to get a phone in every classroom

LONG TERM SOLUTIONS

Ongoing expenses are minimized thanks to VoIP’s easy, simple and browser-based management. Software tools eliminate a lot of physical work reduce reliance on expensive outside services.

The end result is an affordable phone system that equips teachers with the latest communications tools, keeps parents and teachers in closer touch, and satisfies mandates to enhance classroom safety.

These types of additional efficiency allow for expanded savings both in time and money. This means additional resources for administration, faculty, staff and most importantly, students.
