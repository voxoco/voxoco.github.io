INSURANCE

For many insurance agencies looking to modernize their communications systems, the first decision is between a traditional phone service and one based on Voice over Internet Protocol technology. VoIP technology has ushered in a new way of thinking about workplace efficiency through unified communications.

VOXO’s core features and others combine to create a unified communications system, the bringing together of voice, data, and video communications, all of which are managed on a single IT infrastructure that is accessible via in-office equipment, laptops, and a range of mobile devices. This allows you and your agents to deliver service at the place of need without losing the ability to communicate in real-time.

Quick and Easy

VOXO’s phone system can be easily expanded, enabling quick rollout to new locations –  a single branch office, a Statewide agency, or a temporary on-site location. VOXO staff can add phones, change extensions, alter voice mail and reconfigure options and deploy new systems with ease.

A VOXO VoIP system also requires less staff to run, thereby saving on payroll or at the very least freeing up the money so that it can instead be allocated to hire additional specialists or equipment.

Whether a large multi-branch agency, or a small independent office, VOXO has a VOIP solution that can fit your needs.
